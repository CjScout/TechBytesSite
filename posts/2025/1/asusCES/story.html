ASUS at CES 2025

January 15, 2025 by Connor

Both cool and weird stuff!

ASUS had its own share of new product launches. First up, a fragrance mouse with little scent pods. They also showed off the ASUS Zenbook A14, the lightest Microsoft Copilot Ready PC with 32 hours of battery life. ASUS also showed off the second-generation ASUS Zenbook DUO laptop, which features a second stacked display for maximum on-the-go productivity.

Finally, they showed off the ASUS ROG Swift PG27UCDMâ€”the world's first 4k 240hz OLED gaming monitor. The number of times a display updates per second is measured in hertz; your typical office display refreshes at 60 or 75hz, or 60-75 times per second. Most gaming monitors are in the 120-144hz range. While this is not the fastest display on the market, some people were showing off 600hz displays, you hit a point of diminishing returns after 240hz.
