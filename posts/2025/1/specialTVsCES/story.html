Special TVs at CES 2025

January 15, 2025 by Connor

Why are these necessary?

In other TV related news, Samsung announced the Frame TV Pro, with Mini-LED display technology. This will result in improved contrast, brightness, and blacks.

Hisense, on the other hand, revealed 110”, 136”, and 163” Monster TVs. They feature 10,000 nit peak brightnesses, and some are custom-install only. Ten thousand nits is equivalent to 34260 lumens. For context, your average flightlight is about 100 lumens. These are…34 times that.
