Technology For The Consumer

Meet the team behind Tech Bytes

Connor Schuler

Founder and host :)

Hi! I'm Connor. I have a background in computer networking and cybersecurity, and I originally started Tech Bytes in High School, as a podcast for my Senior Capstone project. The podcast aimed to inform consumers about the rapidly changing world of technology. I saw so many examples of misconceptions and out-of-date information creating unhappy consumers, so I decided to do something about it. Since then, its expanded to so much more.

In my spare time, I enjoy listening to music and trying new technologies. I also regularly participate in activities with my Boy Scout troop, even though I earned the rank of Eagle Scout and aged out. I also maintain a personal website, connorthenetworkjester.net.
